Shark Poster

This poster represents an Akhelian Allopex from Warhammer Age of Sigmar. The poster was created in Photoshop, to create the poster I searched the internet to gather graphics and images which relate to the Akhelian Allopex, I also sampled some text from the Age of Sigmar wiki to add some info about the creature to the poster. During the creation I picked the classic 3D glasses colour scheme and leaned into it by adding mirror images of different colours, to different elements for example the main Akhelian Allopex in the square.
