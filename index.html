About Me

I’m a student currently attending Mcast Mosta. Studying an Advanced Diploma in Digital Design, I am currently in its second year. My hobbies are Gaming, Coding, Painting and Designing. As a child I have always been a creative person, which allows me to imagine and create designs/illustrations just from a short description or vague idea.

As an Artists I have always enjoyed drawing on traditional pencil and paper. But as I progressed through my career I started to enjoy using Digital tools more. These Digital tools are Photoshop and Illustrator, which both give me the versatility to create high quality illustrations and designs. I have continued to strive to grow my skills and expertise, always getting better along the way.
