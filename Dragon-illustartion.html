Dragon illustartion

This illustration was created in photoshop for my intro to drawing unit. What I was trying to achieve with this illustration was an interesting perspective. That would try to represent how it would look if you were riding a dragon over a scenic landscape. And I think I managed to achieve my goal with this piece. I love how I managed to achieve both a interesting perspective and composition. This Illustration was also when of the first I was fully proud of as it really showed how I had progressed from my first illustration at the beginning of the year and my final illustration.
