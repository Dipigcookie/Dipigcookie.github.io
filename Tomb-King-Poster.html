Tomb Kings Poster

This poster represents the Tomb Kings faction from the Warhammer Fantasy Battle Universe. To represent the style of grunge I had to print out different artworks of the Tomb kings. Then to make it more authentic I ripped the prints to focus on interesting focal points of the artworks. I scanned the ripped the prints and used Photoshop to put them into a digital poster. Using photoshop I added gradient, colour and saturation filters as well as adding scanned images of tape and chalk strokes to emphasize that effect.
