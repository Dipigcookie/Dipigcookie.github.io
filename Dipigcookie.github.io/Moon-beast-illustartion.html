Moon Beast illustartion

This illustration was done during the summer after the first year of my advanced Diploma. I wanted to practice and gauge the skills I have acquired during that first year attending the course. I wanted to play with how I use perspective. So I chose to base the illustration in space, which allowed me the freedom to basically what I wanted. The illustration represents a space craft being assaulted by two living celestial bodies while surrounded by regular planets in space. I am pretty proud of this work as it shows how I had improved over the course of a few months.
